Spring Cut Plan

Week 1 - Strength and Fat Loss

Exercise Schedule: 7-day fat loss and strength programme

Meal Plan: 1700 kcal, high protein, mostly whole foods

Protein: 135g

Carbs: 155g

Fats: 55g

Cardio: 3 sessions

Target Loss: 0.5 kg/wk

Monday

Lower Body Strength

Squats, Romanian deadlifts, split squats, calves

Tuesday

Zone 2 Cardio

35 minutes bike or incline walk

Wednesday

Upper Body Strength

Bench press, rows, pulldowns, shoulders, arms

Thursday

Recovery

Walking, mobility, stretching

Friday

Full Body Conditioning

Circuit training and light intervals

Saturday

Cardio Intervals

6 intervals with easy recovery between rounds

Sunday

Rest Day

Walking and meal prep

Guidelines

Hit your protein target daily.

Walk after meals when possible.

Sleep 7 to 8 hours.

Keep training logs.

Health Note

Consult your physician before starting.

Stop if you feel chest pain or dizziness.
